Junglee Chicken Pizza

Wednesday, October 5, 2022

REVIEW

RECIPE:

Prep Time: 5 mins
Cooking Time: 25 mins
Makes: 2 servings

INGREDIENTS:

  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • 1 chicken pattie
  • 2 cups of marinara sauce (Trader Joe's)
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • 2 cauliflower torilla sheets
  • 6 Basil leaves
  • Garlic powder
  • Oregano and chiliflakes to taste

COOKING INSTRUCTIONS:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degress.
  2. Add the chicken patties to a pan with cooking oil and let it cook until it browns.
  3. Remove the chicken from the pan and cut into cubes.
  4. Take the torilla's and add marinara sauce to them (coat based on preference).
  5. Add the chicken cubes and sprinkle some garlic powder to the torilla's.
  6. Add vegetables like bell peppers, corn, mushrooms if you prefer.
  7. Add some shredded cheese (coat based on preference).
  8. Top it with some basil leaves and place in the oven at 350 degrees for 10 minutes.
  9. Once, the crust is a golden, brown, garnish with orgeano and chiliflakes.
  10. Enjoy!
