Asian Stir Fry Noodles with Shrimp Dumplings

Tuesday, September 27, 2022

REVIEW

RECIPE:

Prep Time: 10 mins
Cooking Time: 15 mins
Makes: 4 servings

INGREDIENTS:

  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • 3/4 pound boneless skinless chicken breasts cut into thin slices
  • 2 eggs
  • 10 ounces fresh egg noodles pre-cooked
  • 1/2 cup shredded carrots
  • 1 teaspoon garlic minced
  • 2 tablespoons hoisin sauce
  • 2 tablespoons schezwan sauce
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup red bell pepper thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup frozen corn
  • 1/4 cup sliced green onion tops
  • 1/4 cup frozen peas
  • 1/2 teaspoon ginger minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 packet of frozen shrimp and cilantro dumplings (Trader's Joes)
  • Chili Oil

COOKING INSTRUCTIONS:

  1. Preheat the vegetable oil in a large pan over medium high heat.
  2. Add the chicken and season with salt and pepper to taste. Cook for 3-4 minutes per side or until golden brown.
  3. Remove the chicken from the pan. Add the peas, carrots, corn and red bell pepper to the pan and cook for 4-5 minutes or until just softened.
  4. Add the garlic and ginger and cook for 30 seconds.
  5. Add the noodles to the pan, along with the chicken. Toss to combine.
  6. Cook up a 2 egg omelet and scramble it. Add this to the pan.
  7. In a small bowl, whisk together the hoisin sauce, soy sauce and schezwan sauce.
  8. Pour the sauce into the pan along and bring to a simmer. Cook for 1 minute or until sauce is thickened.
  9. Garnish with green onions.
  10. Steam the dumplings in a microwave for 2 minutes. Add chili oil to taste.
  11. Enjoy!
