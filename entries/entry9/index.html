Grilled Chicken Burger

Enjoy a game of tic-tac-toe with a friend!

9.6/10

This is the perfect dish to make on a chill saturday night. It can be paired with some waffle fries and a glass of coca cola. This burger felt healthier because of the chicken being grilled instead of fried and there were a lot of vegetables as well. The juicy chicken with soft buttered bun and the creamy cheese made my mouth water. It was a filling meal, would definitely recommend it if you are craving burgers.
