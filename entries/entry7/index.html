Grilled Salmon with Sauteed Vegetables

Friday, 28 October, 2022

9/10
Grilled salmon is a delicacy in many places, it is fairly simple to make and is one of my favorite things to eat. I made this one in a lemon, butter sauce and it was divine. The butter charred the salmon so well and the vegetables paired with it were mouthwatering. The salmon was a little under cooked, but the vegetables coated in the thyme and garlic were perfect.
