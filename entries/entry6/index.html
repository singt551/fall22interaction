Chicken Shawarma

Tuesday, October 11, 2022

REVIEW

RECIPE:

Prep Time: 10 mins
Cooking Time: 30 mins
Makes: 4 servings

INGREDIENTS:

  • Cooking oil
  • Pre marinated chicken thighs (Trader Joe's)
  • 4 Flour tortilla's
  • Roasted garlic hummus
  • Spicy Tahini
  • Yogurt
  • Romaine lettuce
  • Salt and pepper to taste.
  • Lemon juice
  • 4 mini cucumbers

COOKING INSTRUCTIONS:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degress.
  2. Put the chicken on a baking sheet and oven roast for 20 mins.
  3. Remove the chicken from the oven and cut into slices.
  4. Heat some oil in a pan and lightly warm the flour torillas.
  5. Mix the spicy tahini with some plain yogurt and spread on the torillas. Spread some hummus as well (amount based on personal preference).
  6. Chop some romaine lettuce and cucumbers and add to the torillas.
  7. Add the chicken slices to the torillas.
  8. Vegetables like tomato and onions are optional, as well as shredded cheese.
  9. Add salt, pepper and lemon juice to taste.
  10. Wrap and enjoy!
