Cripsy Chicken Wrap

Arrange your meal as you please!

9.8/10
This wrap was something new I was trying, and the results were amazing. The taste of the soft tortilla with the crunchy chicken tenders, balanced with the barbecue and chick-fil-a sauce was perfect. I would definitely consider adding some vegetables to this like lettuce, cucumber, tomatoes, onions or peppers to make it a little healthier. Overall, this was extremely tasty.
